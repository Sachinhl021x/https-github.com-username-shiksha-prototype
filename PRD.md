# Product Requirements Document (PRD) - Shiksha

## 1. Introduction
**Shiksha** is a specialized platform spun off from the **GenAI4Code** initiative. It focuses exclusively on the **Engineering** and **Research** aspects, along with a dedicated **Chatbot** feature. The goal is to provide a focused environment for developers and researchers, free from the distractions of news, market insights, and general AI product listings.

## 2. Product Goals
- **Focus**: Deliver a clean, engineering-centric user experience.
- **Functionality**: Provide robust tools for engineering documentation, research sharing, and an interactive AI chatbot.
- **Aesthetics**: Retain the premium, high-tech theme of GenAI4Code.

## 3. Key Features
### 3.1 Engineering Section
- Detailed technical documentation and resources.
- Architecture diagrams and implementation guides.
- (Retained from GenAI4Code's `engineering` route)

### 3.2 Research Section
- Academic papers, whitepapers, and research findings.
- (Retained from GenAI4Code's `research` route)

### 3.3 Chatbot
- Interactive AI assistant for coding and technical queries.
- Integrated into the main navigation or as a standalone feature.

## 4. Out of Scope (Removed Features)
- **News**: General AI news and updates.
- **Insights**: Market analysis and trends.
- **AI Products**: Listings of consumer AI tools.
- **General Homepage**: The original marketing-heavy homepage will be replaced by a dashboard or the Engineering landing page.

## 5. User Interface
- **Theme**: Dark mode, glassmorphism, vibrant accents (inherited from GenAI4Code).
- **Navigation**: Simplified sidebar or top bar focusing on Engineering, Research, and Chat.

## 6. Technical Requirements
- **Frontend**: Next.js (React).
- **Backend**: Node.js/Express (if applicable).
- **Database**: PostgreSQL (Prisma) - *To be confirmed if needed for Shiksha specific features*.
